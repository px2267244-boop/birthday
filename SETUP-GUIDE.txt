TO ADD YOUR CONTENT:
-------------------

1. ADD PHOTOS:
   - Open the "images" folder
   - Copy your photos there
   - Rename them: photo1.jpg, photo2.jpg, photo3.jpg, photo4.jpg
   - Supported formats: JPG, PNG

2. ADD VIDEOS:
   - Open the "videos" folder  
   - Copy your video there
   - Rename it: memory1.mp4
   - Supported format: MP4

3. ADD MUSIC:
